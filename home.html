Reflection

         Taking CSIS 1430 Introduction to Web Development helped me solidify my programming skills in making programs and websites. I came in with a lot of coding background but one thing I was lacking was the design aspect of web development. Making programs, playing around with styles, colors and fonts really helped me improve what I was lacking. I came a long way from when I started the course and my websites have improved in design aspects with every new website created as well as making my sites more responsive.

With this type of course, one of the big hurdles is that there are multiple ways to do things and no one solution. To overcome the challenge, you would have to try the solutions one after the other and see which one works. Even though sometimes the process does take a while, it will still get you to your end goal.

Everything I learn applies to what I am doing of finishing up at Salt Lake Community College to get the certificates for web development and eventually transfer to Weber State to obtain my Computer Science Bachelor Degree. After I get my degree I will continue to be a web developer and make projects for clients with their inputs on designs and functionality. Not only will I continue to learn programming, even far after school, but I will also pass on my knowledge to others so they too can perfect the skill.
